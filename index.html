Hello! I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at the University of California, Berkeley, advised by Prof. Max Shen and Prof. Aditya Guntuboyina. Previously I obtained my BS degree in Mathematics from USTC in 2017.

I am primarily interested in developing data-driven methods for decision-making, especially in the presence of strategic or boundedly rational human behaviors. My research interests include nonparametric statistics, mixture modeling, online learning and shape constrained statistical estimation.
